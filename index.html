About Me

Hello! I'm Alex, a dedicated full stack developer who revels in crafting end-to-end solutions that merge the technical and creative aspects of web development. With a strong command of both front-end and back-end technologies, my goal is to create immersive, user-centric digital experiences that leave a lasting impact. My journey into the world of full stack development began in 2019, by the realization that the technologies I was grappling with were hindering rather than enhancing the process. Since then, I've been captivated by the art of transforming ideas into functional, robust applications that people can enjoy to use.

My Approach

My development philosophy centers around collaboration, adaptability, and an unwavering commitment to quality code. Whether I'm architecting databases, crafting responsive front-end interfaces, or optimizing server-side performance, I take pride in my attention to detail and my drive to create experiences that stand the test of time.

The Important Stuff

  • Proficient in both front-end (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React) and back-end (PHP, Node.js, Express) technologies
  • Experience with popular databases like MySQL and MongoDB
  • Familiarity with MVC architecture and API integration
  • Version control with Git and GitHub
  • A passion for clean, scalable code and agile development methodologies
